Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a tomato-based sauce, enriched with butter and cream. It's a popular vegetarian dish that's enjoyed with naan, roti, or rice.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a pan, heat butter and oil. Saut√© onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  2. Season the Sauce: Add garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt. Stir well and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add Paneer: Add the cubed paneer to the sauce and gently mix to coat the paneer with the sauce. Add fenugreek leaves and stir.
  4. Add Paneer: Add the cubed paneer to the sauce and gently mix to coat the paneer with the sauce. Add fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) and stir.
  5. Finish with Cream: Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes, ensuring the paneer is well-coated with the creamy sauce.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
