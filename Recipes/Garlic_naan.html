Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan is a popular Indian flatbread made with garlic and butter. It is typically served with curries, but can also be enjoyed on its own or with dips. The naan is soft, fluffy, and full of flavor, making it a perfect accompaniment to a variety of dishes.

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 4-5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Butter (for brushing)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
    • Add yogurt and milk, and mix to form a soft dough. Knead for 5-10 minutes until smooth.
    • Cover and let it rest for 1-2 hours.
  2. Shape the Naan:
    • Divide the dough into equal portions.
    • Roll each portion into a ball, then flatten into a disc.
    • Sprinkle minced garlic and coriander leaves on top, and gently press them into the dough.
  3. Cook the Naan:
    • Heat a tawa or skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Place the naan on the hot skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
    • Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
    • Brush with melted butter while hot.
  4. Serve:
    • Serve the garlic naan hot with your favorite curry, dip, or as a delicious side.
